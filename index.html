About Us

Welcome to saveetha school of engineering! Where dreams come in full color and leave in black and white. From the outside, it’s a palace; inside, it’s as broken as your semester’s wallet. Here, you either lose your mind, your sleep, or your hope—sometimes all three. It’s beautiful, it’s brutal, and by the end, you’ll be a survivor with a degree and a thousand stories your parents will never hear.
